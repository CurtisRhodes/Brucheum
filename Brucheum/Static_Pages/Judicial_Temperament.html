Judicial Temperament
by:
edit
I'M NOT AN ANGRY DRUNK!! DAMN IT. His nose cherry red, his cheeks blotching up, the blood vessels in his neck straining. As been said this is not an investigation, it's a hearing to determine the fitness of the candidate for the Supreme Court.

I'm not racist. I'm not saying all Irishmen are drunks, only every one I've ever known. "I like beer." "Do you like beer?" "I like beer." He must have said that a dozen times. What was that all about? But the main thing that's sinking in across the nation and not aging well was his anger. He was yelling and crying. He really lost it. He was belligerent, rude, aggressive to Senators.

What stood out was the different face he put on as they alternated every five minutes between Democrat and Republican questioners. When a democrat senator was about to question he put on this purced face of absolute contemptuous hostility. Like the look Maria Sharapova gives to her opponent when she's about to serve. "I will kill you and your entire family and your entire village." He glared at them like a true partican warrior would scowl at their mortal political enemy.

This guy has been in the trenches of the most vicious political warfare any of us have seen. He was there for the Clinton impeachment. He was in Florida for the stealing of the election for W. That granted him entre into the White House where he contributed to the torture memo; signing statements that sidestepped direct congressional legislation. There could be a no more dedicate culture warrior.
