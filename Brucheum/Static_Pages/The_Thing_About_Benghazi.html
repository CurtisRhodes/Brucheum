Conspiracy Theory of the Week
The Thing About Benghazi
Gandolf
edit
No one has ever changed their position on anything it is possible to change
If you ever saw 'Mission Impossible' you know that the show starts with Mr. Phelps receiving his secret assignment. And we all know that right before the tape self-destructs in five seconds it always says "If any of your IM force is caught or captured the Secretary will disavow any knowledge of your actions." By now it's generally known what was going on in Benghazi. Following the decapitation of the Qaddafi regime it seems some MAN-pads came up missing. A MAN-pad is a shoulder fired surface to air missile sort of like a stinger missile from Iran-Contra fame and for the devastating impact stinger missiles had on the Soviets when Charlie Wilson armed the Mujahadeen with them in Afghanistan. Except that a MAN-pad can take out an aircraft at 30,000 feet, which is like six miles which is where commercial airlines fly. It is a state of the art heat seaking proximity weapon which means it doesn't even need to hit its target, just get close enough to explode and blow the tail or nose section off or just sufficiently disrupt the avionics. It seems the US military and the CIA are highly uninterested in having this particular weapons system floating around unaccounted for. It's likely that an operation was underway in Benghazi to police up some of these weapons. Chris Stephens was a full rank State Department ambassador, but he was not the kind of ambassador that gets appointed to Paris or Rome as a political reward for donating to the winning campaign. He was an ambassador in a war zone, which is a much more highly operational role not assigned to amateurs. In some areas he may have had a higher security clearance than Secretary Hillary herself. There is no reason to assume that she was even read into an operation as sensitive as the attempt to reacquire these embarrassingly unaccounted for weapons until the night the mission went sideways. Tripoli is the capital of Libya not Benghazi. Libya, like Iraq, is a nation whose borders are rectangular lines drawn on a map by mid level British officials following the dissolution of the Ottoman empire after World War I . The lines do not reflect the facts on the ground. The western part of Libya where Tripoli is located is more like Algeria or Tunisia. To the east, where Benghazi is located is an entirely different tribe of people more like the Egyptians. (note: my understanding of the ethnic tribes of Libya is 99% inaccurate). They may be Berbers or Carthaginians for all I know. But Benghazi is not Tripoli and the people of each area don't even like each other. The building where Chris Stephens was killed was not the embassy. It may not have even been a formal consulate. It was more of a safe house, unmarked, nondescript in a quiet upscale residential area of the city. The consulate or safe house whatever you want to call it, had a panic room in it which was made of thick steel. When whoever it was who attacked his house, they managed to blow it to bits. I suspect that one of the rebels said; "You want your fancy stinger missiles back? Here I'll give you one back." and fired a MAN-pad into the house. Again, this is not a simple rocket propelled grenade. Not that a RPG would not do serious damage, but a MAN-pad would do several orders of magnitude more. Chis Stephens' panic room turned into an oven and he was effectively baked to death. Meanwhile, at the same time, September 11, 2011, in Cairo Egypt an actual embassy came under attack and was in serious danger of being breached. No offence to Benghazi, but in the scheme of things, what was going on in Cairo had to be considered a much more serious situation. Secretary Clinto n and even the president were woken up and informed of the situation in Cairo. Extra emergency assets were deployed, the rules of engagement were elev ated and a concerted effort to defend the US embassy in Egypt was underway. If I'm not mistaken, other embassies in the Middle East were also coming under attac k. And the situation in Egypt was explicitly understood to be a reaction to an internet video that insulted the prophet Muhammad, at least by the mobs chanting in the streets. In the midst of all of this, reports that something was going on in Benghazi were coming through but may have been lost in the fog. But by late that night the ambassador was already dead. If Clinton even asked what the hell he was doing in Benghazi anyway, she may or may not have been given a full briefing. It was a clandestine mission and the protocol was to deny any knowledge of why he was even there. So no rescue mission was likely contemplated at least until Cario was contained. But if things were not bad enough, as Murphy's Law clearly states, they can always get worse. About eight hours later, the next morning, what has been described as a CIA compound in a warehouse district on the other side of Benghazi came under attack. It has since been suggested that the CIA may have been 'holding' some insurgent rebels at this location. So technically the location could be described as a secret CIA prison. To the aggrieved mother who cried "Why did Hillary lie to me?" remember what Mr. Phelps is told every time just before his secret instructions self-destruct. "If any of your IM forces are caught or killed the Secretary will disavow any knowledge of your actions." The Secretary. The SECRETARY. After all the relentless hatred about her being dishonest I know that I personally would have cracked long ago and spilled the beans about the secret mission and the CIA's rouge behavior. But, good soldier that she is, Hillary has endured countless House committee bloviating rants and perhaps irreparable damage to her reputation. To this day has never mentioned officially publicly any of the now fairly well known details about the missing surface to air missiles or what Chris Stevens and the CIA were up to in Benghazi. Why the coincidence that the attack on the CIA compound happened the day after the killing of the ambassador? Perhaps the location of the CIA black site prison was compromised by his cell phone or some document found at the destroyed home where Chis Stephens was killed. Then the insurgents full of adrenal ine raced off to attack the CIA site trying to release their captured comrades. Some hapless non military type computer guy was shot and killed, the son o f the mother so horrendously exploited by Fox News and Trump. But then they met resistance. Some Blackwater contractors on the roof in a snipers nest held off the attack. Then someone fired a mortar. Mortar tubes are not the most accurate of weapons but somehow they landed a round right on top of the roof. Depending on how far away they were from the building they had to fire the round at almost a 90 degree angle to hit those two Blackwater contractors on the roof. That was a hell of a shot. Again, as per the CIA's rules of engagement as so often repeated to Mr. Phelps, when your cover is blown, it's over. Your on your own. Expect no help to come. That's how the game is played. This is not the Marines leave no man behind. Your orders are to destroy everything, especially hard drives and any secret co mmunications equipment, shred all documents then bend over and kiss your ass goodbye. I don't know if an evacuation mission was eventually performed. There may have been more deaths than reported. Blackwater security may have been able to hold the position long enough to repel the attack. Only Clay Gouddy knows. Again, its unclear that Hillary was even aware of the Benghazi operation until the night it all went Fubar. Even so, even if she were a man, what would you want the Secretary of State to tell that aggrieved mother at Dover AFB a few days later? Would you want her to read her in to all these details or would you expect her to do her job? "Your son was working at a secret CIA black site prison in a war zone that was compromised by an op eration to police up missing surface to air missiles." I suspect the kid told his mother he was working in Vermont or something.
